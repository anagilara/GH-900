## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1. **Do not create a public issue.**
2. Email us at **security@example.com** (replace with your actual security contact).
3. Include:
   - A clear description of the vulnerability.
   - Steps to reproduce the issue.
   - Any potential impact.

We will acknowledge your report within ***48 hours*** and provide updates as we work on a fix.

## Disclosure Policy
- Please allow us **90 days** to address the issue before public disclosure.
- We may request additional time for complex fixes.
